scan helps to scan the open ports of target.

  just specify the target
  and select the mode of scan.
  modes:
    1: scan for open port from 1 - 1024
    2: scan for open port from 1 - 49152
    3: scan for open port in 20,21,22,23,25,53,80,110,443
    4: provide custom port numbers to scan (seprate by blank)
          ex- 20 78 95 06
    5: to exit the tool
